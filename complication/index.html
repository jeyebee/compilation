Course We Offer

Intermediate

The Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus is a branch of Cebu Technological University. It provides both undergraduate and graduate programs and is recognized as the Center for Internationalization.

Degree

Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus provides both undergraduate and graduate degrees, which include: Undergraduate degrees: Bachelor of Science in Industrial Engineering (BSIE) Bachelor of Science in Mechanical Engineering (BSME) Graduate degrees: Master in Public Administration (MPA) Master of Arts in Vocational Education (MAVed) Master of Science in Industrial Technology (MSIT)

Post Graduation

The Cebu Technological University (CTU) Danao Campus provides a variety of graduate programs, such as: Master in Public Administration (MPA) Master of Arts in Vocational Education (MAVed) Master of Science in Industrial Technology (MSIT) To qualify for the comprehensive exam, graduate students at CTU must fulfill these criteria: Master's students: Completion of 30 units of coursework and submission of all grades Doctorate students: Completion of 48 units of coursework and submission of all grades
