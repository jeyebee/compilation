Delicious and Affordable: Exploring the CTU Canteen

By John Benedict Valmores | Published on 11/29/24

Streamlining Campus Security: ID Scanning for CTU Students

By John Benedict Valmores | Published on 12/29/24

Cebu Technological University (Danao Campus) has introduced an efficient and secure system for managing student entry and exit from the campus. The newly implemented ID scanning process not only enhances security but also simplifies record-keeping for both students and administrators.

Upon arriving at any CTU campus, students are required to scan their ID cards at the designated scanners located at the entrance. This process records their log-in time in the system. Similarly, students must scan their ID again when leaving the campus to register their log-out time.The system operates in real time, providing the university with accurate data on campus occupancy and ensuring that only authorized individuals have access.
